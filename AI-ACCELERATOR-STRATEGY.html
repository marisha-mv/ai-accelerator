‚ö†Ô∏è Risks & Mitigation Strategies

Risk #1: Low Webinar Show-Up Rate

Risk: Only 20-30% of registrants show up live

Mitigation:

  • Send 3 reminder emails (24h, 1h, 10min before)
  • SMS reminders for high-intent leads
  • Make registration more friction-full (phone number required) to increase commitment
  • Offer valuable pre-webinar content to build anticipation
  • Replay available but emphasize "live-only bonuses"

Risk #2: Low Conversion Rate

Risk: Less than 5% of webinar attendees buy

Mitigation:

  • A/B test webinar pitch (timing, price reveal, scarcity)
  • Strengthen social proof (more testimonials, case studies)
  • Test different progressive pricing intervals (12h vs 24h vs 48h)
  • Extended email nurture sequence post-webinar
  • Retargeting ads with different angles

Risk #3: Technical Issues During Workshop

Risk: Zoom crashes, participants can't build clones, tools don't work

Mitigation:

  • Dry run workshop 1 week before with test group
  • Tech support team on standby during all sessions
  • Backup Zoom accounts ready to switch
  • Record everything (cloud + local backup)
  • Clear troubleshooting docs shared in advance
  • Slack/Discord channel for real-time tech help

Risk #4: Participants Don't Build Working Clones

Risk: People struggle to build clones, leave disappointed

Mitigation:

  • Build Sprint with 1-on-1 facilitator support
  • Pre-workshop setup checklist sent 1 week before
  • Breakout rooms for peer support
  • Simplify build process (step-by-step templates)
  • Office hours after workshop for stragglers
  • 30-day access to facilitators for Q&A

Risk #5: High Refund Rate

Risk: 10-20% refund rate tanks profitability

Mitigation:

  • Set expectations clearly (not magic, requires effort)
  • Qualify leads better (webinar filters for serious buyers)
  • Over-deliver on value (exceed expectations)
  • Proactive check-ins during workshop
  • Exit survey for refund requests to improve future cohorts
